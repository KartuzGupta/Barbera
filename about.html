    Our story

    It all started when our team members were preparing for JEE Advanced, the exam which is believed to be the toughest in the country.
    Students leave everything to get admission in IITs, even the sleep time reduces drastically. At this crucial time waiting in a salon for three hours to have your haircut done is not at all a good decision.
    But nothing else can be done, having a haircut is one of the necessary things which can not be avoided.
    From here the concept of BARBERA came.
    All we need is a Salon which can provide us with expert services at the comfort of our home so that not must time is wasted in waiting in the shops.
    With a lot of effort and hard work, this idea of an online salon was brought on the field with the name BARBERA.
    Today Barbera has satisfied more than 2500+ customers with expert services and continues working hard to win the hearts of the whole country.
